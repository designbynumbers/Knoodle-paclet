# Very hard unknot diagrams

Three of the reinforcement-learning-generated hard unknot diagrams from:

> Taylor Applebaum, Sam Blackwell, Alex Davies, Thomas Edlich, András
> Juhász, Marc Lackenby, Nenad Tomašev, and Daniel Zheng, "The Unknotting
> Number, Hard Unknot Diagrams, and Reinforcement Learning", *Experimental
> Mathematics*, 2025. DOI: 10.1080/10586458.2025.2542174, arXiv:2409.09032.

These are the three largest diagrams (by crossing number) among the paper's
published examples: #257 (59 crossings), #216 (51 crossings), and #240
(48 crossings), from the dataset the authors published alongside the paper.
Format: Knoodle native signed PD code, one crossing per row, five
tab-separated integers (four 0-based arc labels plus handedness ±1).

Every diagram in this directory is the unknot. They are bundled as example
data for the `KnoodleSimplify` documentation.
